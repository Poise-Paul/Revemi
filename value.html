Value chain

Production

Includes all of the activities involved in the production of raw food materials, such as crop and livestock commodities.

Processing

Products might undergo further processing to create by-products.

Storage

Monitoring and providing storage/aggregation unit for products.

Distribution

Monitoring production, tracking products in transit and systems along the value chain to aggregate quality products for distribution.

Marketing

Expanding the market reach for local farmers and deemed exporters.

Consumer

The terminal stage in the food and agribusiness value chain.

Information Sharing

Data gathering, data analysis and data sharing across the ecosystem to ensure robust quality control and assurance standards.
